Last updated: August 13, 2025

Overview

FD RD Calculator ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles information when you use our service.

Information We DON'T Collect

We want to be completely transparent: FD RD Calculator does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Specifically, we do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Financial information or calculation data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Crash reports or diagnostic information
  • Any other personal or sensitive information

How the App Works

  • Local Processing: All calculations are performed entirely on your device
  • No Data Storage: Your input values and calculation results are not saved permanently
  • No Internet Required: The app works completely offline
  • No Accounts: No user registration or login required
  • No Tracking: We do not track your usage or behavior

Permissions

FD RD Calculator requires no special Android permissions. The app does not access:

  • Your contacts or phone book
  • Your location or GPS
  • Your camera or microphone
  • Your storage or files
  • Your network or internet connection
  • Any other device features

Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services, including:

  • Analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • Advertising networks
  • Crash reporting services
  • Cloud storage services
  • Social media integrations

Data Security

Since we don't collect any data, there is no data to secure or breach. All your calculations remain private on your device.

Children's Privacy
